Who We Are

Black Cat Coffee Roasters is a small, independent specialty coffee roaster based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Black Cat Coffee was founded by Sonia Tire in 2017 due to her passion about roasting and crafting signature espresso beverages. After being a barista for 9 years Sonia wanted to elevate the coffee experiene for her community and thus created Black Cat. We are dedicated to create the perfect sip for each and every costumer that walks in our doors. We create a warm and inviting environement for all. We offer quality and presonalized drinks for every taste. Come and visit us today!
